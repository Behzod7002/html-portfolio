Information message Ministry of Higher and Secondary Special Education of the Republic of Uzbekistan Research Institute of Semiconductor Physics and Microelectronics and the Faculty of Physics of the National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek

Decree of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 178 of March 6, 2018 at the Scientific Research Institute of Semiconductor Physics and Microelectronics and the Faculty of Physics Mirzo Ulugbek National University of Uzbekistan is held on October 26-27, 2018 Republican Conference "Modern Problems of Semiconductor Physics". The conference invites professors, researchers, researchers, researchers, independent applicants, undergraduates and gifted students of higher educational institutions and research institutes of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan and all interested ministries and organizations.

As part of the conference program, plenary and breakout sessions are held in the following areas.
  • Section 1: Problems of Semiconductor Physics
  • Section 2: Semiconductor Materials
  • Section 3: Problems of nano- and microelectronics of semiconductors
  • Section 4: Heliophysics and the use of alternative energy sources
  • Section 5: Theoretical and Radiation Physics of Solids
  • Section 6: The use of devices based on semiconductor materials in engineering and industry.
