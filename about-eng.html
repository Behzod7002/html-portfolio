On the establishment of the Research Institute of Semiconductor Physics and Microelectronics under National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek

In order to ensure the high-quality fulfillment of the program tasks defined in the resolutions of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan dated July 1, 2017 No. PP-3151 “On measures to further expand the participation of industries and economic sectors in improving the quality of training of specialists with higher education” and No. PP dated November 1, 2017 -3365 "On measures to further strengthen the infrastructure of research institutions and the development of innovative activities", as well as conducting deep fundamental and applied research in the field of physics, the development of inn based on the needs of the real sectors of the country's economy and the wide involvement of young scientists and students in the scientific process on June 26, 2019, the Cabinet of Ministers decided to create, on the basis of the Laboratory of Semiconductor Physics and Microelectronics, the Physics Department of the Mirz Ulugbek National University of Uzbekistan, the Scientific Research Institute of Semiconductor Physics and microelectronics at the National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek.

The main objectives of the Institute are:

- participation in the formation of priority areas and fundamental and applied research, implementation of innovative projects in the field of semiconductor physics, semiconductor microelectronics, materials science, heliophysics and alternative energy sources, including by organizing cluster form of research projects, as well as analysis of current world trends in the development of the industry of physics of semiconductors and microelectronics;

- the development of technologies for creating new semiconductor materials and the study of the parameters of multilayer structures based on them, which are the basic elements of microelectronics and photonics;

- creation of modern semiconductor devices of various functional purposes with stable and reproducible parameters, which are in demand in high-tech spheres and sectors of the economy of the republic;

- strengthening the Institute’s material and technical base with modern educational and scientific laboratory equipment necessary for conducting research and implementing innovative projects in the field of semiconductor physics and semiconductor microelectronics;

-the development and implementation of measures for the interaction of higher educational institutions, research institutions, economic management bodies, industrial and other interested organizations, as well as the private sector, aimed at introducing innovations in the field of semiconductor physics and semiconductor microelectronics;
- ensuring the integration of fundamental and applied science with the educational process at all stages, including the use of a laboratory base for the implementation of educational, experimental, laboratory and term papers, industrial and undergraduate practice;

- improving the quality and level of educational and methodical work by assisting in the creation of new curricula, textbooks, teaching aids in relevant areas of undergraduate education and master's degrees;

- the establishment and development of international relations, the organization of the development and implementation of joint international projects in the field of physics of semiconductors and semiconductor microelectronics, as well as international scientific conferences and symposia;

- the provision of scientific and methodological assistance in the preparation of bachelors and masters, as well as the preparation of scientific and pedagogical personnel of higher qualification by organizing training at post-graduate education institutes on the basis of the Institute in the relevant specialties of physics;

- the development of high technology and the introduction of innovative developments in the field of physics of semiconductors, microelectronics and photonics in production, as well as the commercialization of research results in accordance with the needs of organizations and sectors of the economy of the republic.
